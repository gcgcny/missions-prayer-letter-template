This Christmas break, I'm joining our team!
We're leaving on December 20th, and arriving back on January 5th.

Our goal is to help the local church share just the gospel message:

  • that God loves us, sent his son Jesus to die for our salvation (John 3:16)
  • that we may have life to the full in Jesus Christ (John 10:10)
We aren't there to share our culture, practices, or customs.

How did I end up joining the missions team?

Although our church has been sending teams for years, I've always been hesitant to join and I had a variety of excuses! I always thought that since I'm not the most eloquent nor extroverted, that evangelism would be a task better suited for someone else.

This past year, God's been showing me that not only has he been working powerfully through the short-term missions trips, but that he called each team member individually to do what he intended.

As our church prepared for this year's trip, I was continually bothered by the question: if God specifically told me to go, would I follow? I committed in prayer that if God wanted me to go, then I am willing.

And so here we are! I'm not sure what to expect, but I have confidence knowing that God is faithful.

Why Northlandia?

Out of Northlandia's population,
only 5.8% are Christian.

This translates to 4 out of every 5 people
who will never encounter a Christian in their lifetime.

It is a privilege to go and share the Gospel to this nation and to build up the local pastors and church.

What we'll be doing

1
Joining in village evangelism efforts
2
Encouraging, supporting, & affirming local Christians, pastors, and evangelists
3
Equipping & empowering the local Christian leaders by sharing God's word with them
